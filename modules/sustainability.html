What is Sustainability?

Key Takeaways

  • Sustainable development meets present needs without compromising future generations.
  • It is based on three pillars: economic growth, social inclusion, and environmental protection.
  • These pillars are interconnected and must be addressed together.
  • It tackles global challenges like poverty, inequality, and climate change.
  • The United Nations promotes sustainable development through global cooperation.
  • 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) guide efforts for global sustainability.
  • Everyone—governments, businesses, and individuals—plays a role in achieving the SDGs.
  • Specific goals include clean water, clean energy, and quality education.
  • Everyday choices and actions can contribute to sustainable development.
  • With collective effort, a sustainable and prosperous future is achievable.
